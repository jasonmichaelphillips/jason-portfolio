Bull vs. Bear

Bull
  • Leveraged BTC accumulation = compounding engine. 818K BTC, 22% YTD growth.
  • mNAV compressed near 1× — de-risked entry; structural premium can re-expand.
  • Software still profitable ($124M Q1, +12% YoY); funds dividend obligations.
  • 90% of analysts bullish · +76% to consensus · +204% to high.
  • Wave (3) base potentially in place; technicals firming.
Bear
  • Leverage cuts both ways: 1.30 beta to BTC, 69% annualized vol, –59% drawdown.
  • +50% diluted-share growth in 15 months — persistent equity dilution.
  • ~$10.6B preferred dividend stack — fixed cash drag even if BTC sinks.
  • $14.5B Q1 unrealized loss; deferred-tax-asset valuation allowance flagged.
  • Death cross intact; BTC could spend more time in $60–90K range.

Key risks

  • BTC price dominance. Bitcoin drives >90% of asset value. MSTR has high beta and correlation to it.
  • Capital structure. Convertible debt + multiple preferred classes create fixed claims senior to common stock.
  • Volatility. 69% annualized realized vol — position sizing matters.
  • Accounting. FASB ASU 2023-08 fair-value rule causes large mark-to-market swings in reported earnings.
  • Substitution risk. Spot BTC ETFs reduce structural reasons for an MSTR premium.
